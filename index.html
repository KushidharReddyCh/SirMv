Why SIR MV PU?

Sir M Visvesvaray is an Institute of repute in the state of Karnataka. The College was established in the year 2009 has crossed many mile stones after sweat and toil, achievements and many hurdles. We believe that “The secret of getting ahead is persistence and perseverance”. As said in bible “Blessed are the humble for in them lies the kingdom of heaven” in spite of hurdles we continued our work in every walk of our life in a humble way.
